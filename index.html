About Algeria

Algeria is a country in North Africa. It is the biggest country in Africa. It has a long coast on the Mediterranean Sea. Algeria has deserts, mountains, and beaches.

The capital citty is Algiers. Most people speak Arabic and French. Algeria has a rich history and beautiful places to visit.

Famous Places in Algeria

  • Algiers - the capital city
  • Sahara Desert - the biggest desert in the world
  • Timgad - Roman ruins
  • Djemila - ancient Roman city

Algerian Food

Algerian food is delicious. It comes from from Arabic, Berber, and French cooking. Let me show you some popular dishes of Algeria:

  1. Couscous - steamed semolina with meat and vegetables
  2. Bread El-kesra
  3. Baklava - sweet pastry with nuts
  4. Makroud - date-filled cookies
